How It Works

Youcert connects Creators and Learners in a seamless loop of knowledge and certification. Find out how the platform works for you.

For Creators

Turn your educational content into a verifiable course and a new revenue stream in just a few simple steps.

1. Sign Up & Verify

Sign up instantly with your Google account. We automatically verify your channel ownership via OAuth to ensure security.

2. Submit Your Link

Go to your Creator Dashboard and paste the YouTube video link you want to turn into an exam.

3. Process Content

Press "Process." Our AI securely collects the transcript and generates a comprehensive exam based on your content.

4. Review Exam

Your draft exam is now listed in your dashboard. Review the AI-generated questions and make any desired edits.

5. Set Price

Once you're happy with the exam, set the price for learners to enroll.

6. Activate & Earn

Activate the exam to publish it to the Youcert marketplace. Now your audience can get certified, and you can earn!

For Learners

Your journey from dedicated viewer to a certified professional is simple, fast, and verifiable.

1. Log In

Sign in securely with your Google account. No new passwords to remember, just one-click access.

2. Find Your Exam

Search for your favorite Creator or the title of the YouTube video or playlist you just completed.

3. Enroll in Exam

Select the exam you want to take and complete the secure purchase to enroll.

4. Take the Exam

Start the timed exam and test your knowledge. Show what you've learned from the content.

5. Get Certified

Upon passing, you'll instantly receive your verifiable, shareable Youcert certificate to post on your portfolio or LinkedIn!
