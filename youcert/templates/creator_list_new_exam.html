Add New Exam from YouTube

Paste a YouTube video or playlist link to automatically generate an exam course.

Single Video Tutorial

Paste the link to a single YouTube video. The system will process this video to generate one complete exam focused on its content.

Example: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ

Entire Playlist Course

Paste the entire playlist URL. The system will process all videos in that playlist to create a comprehensive multi-part course exam.

Example: https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLB-dY9d6yO23tLz091a1N9410d-jC1vYk
