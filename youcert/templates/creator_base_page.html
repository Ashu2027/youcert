Common Questions

Is it free for me as a creator?

Yes. You don’t pay subscriptions or setup fees. Your learners only pay when they registers for an exam.

How do I create an exam?

It's a simple 3-step process: 1) Sign in with Google, 2) paste a YouTube video or playlist link and let AI generate questions, 3) Set a price and activate the exam.

Can I edit the AI-generated questions?

For now, NO. As this is a beta version, you can't do it right now due to our limitation. but later the feature will be available.

How do I get paid?

You earn a share from each exam purchased. We handle the infrastructure. You will receive 65%, while we will keep 35% to run the entire operation and development smoothly.
