Help Center & FAQ

Welcome to the Youcert Help Center. Find answers to common questions about our platform. We've divided this guide into sections for Learners and Creators.

For Learners

Account & Getting Started

Creating an account is simple and secure. We use Google OAuth, which means you just click the "Sign In" button and log in with your existing Google account. There are no new passwords to remember, and we never see your Google password.

Finding & Purchasing Exams

After logging in, you can use the search bar on your dashboard. You can search by the Creator's name, the YouTube video title, or the YouTube playlist title. If a Creator has created an exam for that content, it will appear in the search results.

If you can't find an exam, it means the Creator of that video has not created one on our platform yet. We recommend contacting the Creator on their YouTube channel or social media and letting them know you'd love for them to offer certified exams on Youcert!

We use a secure third-party payment gateway (like Razorpay or Stripe) to handle all transactions. Once you click "Enroll," you will be guided through a secure checkout process where you can use various payment methods like credit/debit cards, UPI, or net banking, depending on your region.

For Creators

Account & Verification

We use the official Google OAuth 2.0 system. When you sign up as a Creator, we will ask you to log in with the Google account that manages your YouTube channel. This is a secure process handled by Google. You are simply giving us permission to confirm that you are the owner of the channel. We never see your password, and we only request the minimum permissions needed to verify your channel ownership.

This is our most important security feature. Verification is mandatory to protect your intellectual property. It ensures that only you, the actual owner of your YouTube channel, can create and sell exams based on your content. This prevents fraud and protects your revenue stream.

Creating Exams

For our AI to generate a high-quality exam, the YouTube video must have clear and accurate transcripts or captions. Auto-generated captions from YouTube work well if the audio is clear, but manually reviewed or uploaded captions are always better. Payments & Revenue

You can securely add your bank account or other payout details in the "Payouts" or "Settings" section of your Creator Dashboard. All information is encrypted and handled in compliance with financial regulations.

We process Creator payouts on a monthly basis (e.g., Net-30, meaning you are paid 30 days after the end of the month in which you earned the revenue). Payouts are typically sent directly to your registered bank account. All details about revenue share and payout schedules are available in your Creator Agreement.
